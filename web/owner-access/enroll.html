Enroll a passkey

Bind this device's secure enclave (Touch ID, Windows Hello, Android keystore) to your forty-two-watts instance. Once enrolled, this device can sign in from anywhere through the relay.

Shows up in the trusted-devices list so you can identify and revoke this passkey later.

Leave blank if you already have a passkey, or if you're enrolling on the local network. For the very first passkey via the relay, read the PIN on the Pi at /api/owner-access/enroll-pin (or its logs).
